Oatmeal

Oat meal is easy and simple to make. When I'm in a hurry oatmeal is my go to meal.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil 1/2 Water
  2. Dump 1/4 of the water onto your oatmeal
  3. Add 1/4 milk to your oatmeal
  4. Add desire amount of sugar
  5. Enjoy

Cup Noodles

There is the basic way to eat a Cup Noodles and then there is the stacked way on how to eat a Cup Noodles. I will be showing you how i eat my cup noodles

Ingredients

  • Cup Noodles
  • ES Cheese
  • Tapatio
  • Lime
  • 2 eggs
  • Smoke Suasage

Steps

  1. Boil 1 cup Water
  2. Wash your eggs and throw them in the boiling water. We want to clean the outer shell
  3. Once boil dump it into your cup of noodels
  4. Add all you ingredients
  5. Enjoy
